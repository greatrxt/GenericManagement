FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We've got answers. If you have some other questions, see our support center.

Is my business data safe & secure?

Absolutely. Each piece of information entered by you is encrypted before it is transmitted to the server, thereby ensuring that your data is private and confidential. We perform regular anti-virus and hack scans to thwart any attempts of data loss by phishing, malware, brute force entries, or others techniques. Quant also offers daily backups of your precious business data, which can be restored in case of any contingencies.

We don’t have any prior experience of working on ERPs, especially cloud-based ones. What if we need support?

Quant has been designed keeping in mind the untrained, uninitiated users so that they can master the system in no-time. We bet you will find the system easy to use and simple to manage. However, we do offer dedicated on-premise support for your team, free-of-cost, so that the users are sufficiently trained and are familiar with their respective workflows. We also offer 24x7 online support to all our customers at no extra cost.

Suppose I sign up for Quant in the middle of the year, what happens to my existing account balances?

You can update the existing Account balances in the Opening Balances section any time. We can also migrate your existing balances into the system for you via MS Excel upload if needed, free-of-charge. You can also customise the Chart of Accounts to suit your existing accounting practices. We are available round-the-clock to assist you.

Does it offer Indian GST portal compliant GSTR reports?

Yes. GSTR-1, GSTR-2 and GSTR-3B reports are auto-generated by the system on a real-time basis. You may upload the same on the GST portal via the Offline Utility Tool provided by the Indian Government (https://www.gst.gov.in/download/returns) or view/export the status anytime from the system, at your convenience.

Does the subscription charge depend on the number of users or is it per license basis?

We offer "Pay as you grow" option so that you can plan your subscription based on your business and manpower needs, and add new users on-the-go. Thus, you pay only for the active users on your system which can be upgraded or downgraded at any time. Full access to the primary modules of Quant like Sales, Purchase, Inventory, and others is provided to all organisations irrespective of the number of subscribed users.

What if I need customisations in the modules. Can you build it for me?

We offer customisation in the system on a case-to-case basis. If you like Quant and would like to have a customised system built for you, please share your details here and we will connect with you on priority.

Where can I see the product and its features?

We offer a free-of-cost product demo at your premise, at your convenience. You may leave your details here and we will get in touch with you on priority.

Before I sign up, I think I would like to try it out to be sure. Do you offer a trial?

Yes. We offer a 10-day free, no-commitment trial if you need. You can write in to us at connect@quanterp.com or drop a message on +91 8291 817 212, and we will organise a free trial for you.
